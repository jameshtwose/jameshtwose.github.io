WHO AM I?

Current Research Master of Behavioural Science student, researching the link between psychopathy, empathy and moral decision making by constructing a moderation model of the constructs of interest and by capturing the dynamics of Galvanic Skin Response whilst rating moral images. The analytical techniques being employed in this research are a Structural Equation Model (SEM), and Recurrence Quantification Analysis (RQA). Both of these analyses are being run in R, due to the Research Master teaching statistics predominantly in this software. I would love to be accepted for a Behavioural Science Joint PhD scholarship, because I would really enjoy following my passion of capturing the dynamics of facial expressions to better understand how we portray, and try to hide, emotion.
